Daily Challenges:

1. Go for a Walk Without Your Phone

Challenge:

Start a Conversation with Someone New

Steps to Complete:

  1. Spot Someone New: Look around your day for someone you haven't met yet.
  2. Break the Ice: Start with a simple opener like, “Hi, how’s your day going?” or “I like your [item], where’s it from?”
  3. Be Curious: Ask an interesting question like, “What’s something fun you’ve done recently?” or “Where would you go if you could travel anywhere?”
  4. Share a Smile: End with a smile and a friendly farewell — small connections matter!
